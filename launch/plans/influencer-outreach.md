# Influencer Outreach Plan

## Targets

- Productivity creators who review small apps.
- Math-study creators who need fast visual examples.
- Cyberpunk/design accounts that post interface experiments.
- Indie app launch newsletters.

## Message

CyberCalc is a lightweight neon calculator with local sync, AR fallback mode, and community challenges. The ask is a 20-second demo clip or a short post showing a calculation and a shared challenge.

## Sequence

1. Send a concise preview with screenshots and a demo link.
2. Offer a custom community challenge for the creator's audience.
3. Follow up once with a new visual asset or launch-event reminder.

## Guardrail

No paid influencer spend is authorized from the repo. Paid outreach requires owner approval.

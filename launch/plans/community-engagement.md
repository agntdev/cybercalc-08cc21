# Community Engagement Strategy

## Loop

1. Publish a daily calculation challenge.
2. Let users share a result with the built-in share payload.
3. Highlight safe, moderated submissions.
4. Use beta feedback to pick the next challenge or visual theme.

## Weekly Cadence

- Monday: new theme prompt.
- Wednesday: speed calculation challenge.
- Friday: community result roundup.

## Moderation

Use local moderation terms from `src/community.js` before generating share links. External community posting stays manual until a real backend and moderation review queue exist.
